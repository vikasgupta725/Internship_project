Analytics

The fundamental idea is to understand that data alone, no matter how complete it is, is useless. The key is to know how to extract real information out of it. As an online store owner, without knowing which marketing activities are working, you will be wasting both time and money. Our Analytics Services will help you in decision making and having a clear cause-effect relationship.

CONVERSION RATE

The main metric for e-commerce is the percentage of visits that end up buying. Vanity metrics (Facebook likes, number of visits to your website, number of clicks on your products’ cards) don’t add any value on their own, but when crossed with another, they do like visits/sales.

We often spend too much effort on increasing traffic when the actual key factor is optimizing the conversion rate.

AVERAGE CHECKOUT PRICE

How much money does a person spend on average from your e-commerce? This is a piece of data that not many people take into account, but it is also crucial. This service will help you to have a close look at the average checkout price of your e-commerce business and add value to the analysis.

CART ABANDONMENT RATE

A buying process that takes too long or has hidden costs normally turns into an abandoned cart. Even though the checkout is becoming easier, there will always be someone who will not complete the purchase. It can’t be avoided, but one should know the percentage.

A high abandoned cart rate may mean that the buying process is too difficult or that there are distracting elements in the way.
