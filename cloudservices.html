Cloud Services

Our Cloud Services can provide data backup, big data analytics, and customer-facing web applications. Cloud services consist of technologies providing infrastructure services like databases, machine learning, and much more. We also provide elastic scalability with your business requirements which reduces the cost of infrastructure and other capital expenses.

WAREHOUSE MANAGEMENT

SaaS-based WMS is now a reality. Our WMS service can help in adding new business partners and suppliers as needed, to expand into new markets and geographies. Our WMS service can schedule and manage inbound shipments, cross-docking, and flow-through allocations.

This service helps you to connect to all major e-commerce channels, such as Amazon, eBay, or other third-party, front-end solutions.

COST MANAGEMENT

Our Cloud Service helps in quickly identifying unused resources that should be shut down, underutilized resources that should be rightsized, and instances for which Reserved Instances would have better rates.

Our service will help in accurately allocate your cloud spend across business units, products, cost centers, and roles. This gives finance the ability to chargeback and operations the ability to optimize resources.

SERVERLESS COMPUTING

Our Cloud Service provides a code execution model in which the cloud provider fully manages starting and stopping virtual machines as necessary to serve requests. These requests are billed by an abstract measure of the resources required to satisfy the request, rather than per virtual machine, per hour.
