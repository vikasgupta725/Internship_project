Payment Gateways

Every e-commerce platform in the world offers secure online payment as a fundamental feature. In no time, online payments have become a usual deal for an e-commerce customer. Along with traditional debit and credit card payments, customers are now transacting though newer options like netbanking, wallets, and UPI.

PCI-DSS WALLET

For recurring payments, we offer PCI-DSS wallets. This enables secure storage of personal data of the user in the portal or gateway. This gateway also helps in securing this personal data against any cybersecurity threat.

This compliance saves your customer from entering personal details again and again. Your customer would always visit your store again.

WHITE-LABEL WALLET

Some payment gateways allow you to make digital transactions through mobile wallet apps. This is the current trend, as it enables the user to make all his transactions by just sitting in one place.

This is a straight tunnel from the gateway to your bank account. Hassle-free movement of funds into the account. Collect transaction receipts in real time and save your overhead.

FRAUD SCREENING TOOLS

Many payment gateways provide you with fraud screening tools to reduce the risk of losing information. These tools include the Card Code Value (CCV), Card Verification Value (CVV), or even the Address Verification Service (AVS). These tools ensure that there is no fraudulent transaction.

Your transactions are safe with us. We ensure that no customer gets cheated on and your funds remain secure. Our specialized security solutions await your subscription.
